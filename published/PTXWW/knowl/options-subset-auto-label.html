Checkpoint 1.6.4. Choose a Subset of Options with Automated Labeling.

  4. \(\displaystyle e^{ (x^3+1) / x}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{ e^{x^2} }{ e^x }\)
  6. None of the above
